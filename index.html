Delete Your Account

To delete your Foil account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Foil app on your device
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear) in the top right
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm by tapping "Delete" in the confirmation dialog

What data will be deleted:

  • Your account credentials
  • All saved food items
  • Your app preferences and settings
  • Notification preferences

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

If you cannot access the app or need assistance, please email us at:
getfoilapp@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your account. Deletion requests are typically processed within 48 hours.
