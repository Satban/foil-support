Everything Your Kitchen Needs

From the grocery store to the dinner table, Foil keeps everything organized and everyone in sync.

AI-Powered

From Receipt to Fridge in Seconds

Snap a photo of your grocery receipt. Foil reads every item, assigns smart expiry dates based on what it actually is, and sorts everything into the right place. No typing required.

  • Recognizes items from any grocery store
  • Tailored expiry dates—spinach gets 4 days, apples get 21
  • Auto-sorts to Fridge, Freezer, Pantry, or Counter
Smart Recipes

Recipes Built Around What You Have

No more staring at the fridge wondering what to make. Foil suggests recipes that prioritize ingredients you should use soon—so everything gets its moment.

  • Prioritizes ingredients that need attention
  • Filter by cuisine, course, and dietary needs
  • Save your favorites to a personal cookbook
Plan Ahead

Plan the Week, Not Just Tonight

Map out breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks for the entire week. Hosting a gathering? The party planner helps you build menus, scale for guests, and create shopping lists automatically.

  • Weekly calendar for all meals
  • Party mode with guest count scaling
  • Auto-generates shopping lists from your plan
Together

Your Kitchen, In Sync

Create a household and invite your family or roommates. Everyone sees the same inventory, updated in real-time. When someone uses the last of the milk, everyone knows.

  • Shared inventory, updated in real-time
  • Activity log shows who added or used items
  • Up to 6 household members
